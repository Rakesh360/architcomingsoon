ABOUT US

Paan chewing is an age old habit in India that has played a significant role in social mores and customs for centuries to the point where a leaf of paan (Betel Leaves) is provided as a part of hospitality. Paan had the advantage of being able to be offered at any moment, but carrying one about was tough. The Pan Masala sector has emerged as a handy option for wet Paan as a result of advancement in food technology and packaging. J S Enterprises created Fehu Archiit Pan Masala, a premium offering to appease the discerning Paan enthusiasts who seek the ideal taste of their good old Paan in dry form, in 2020 to perpetuate the Paan heritage. Fehu Archiit has given its users the freedom to travel with, stays with, and utilize their favourite flavours whenever and whe rever they want. The brand aims at the higher end of market, selling premium Paan Masala. The brand strong distribution network is backed up by knowledgeable agents, dealers and retailers all over the country. Since its inception, we have been built on t he foundation of perfection and quality. Fehu Archiit is known for its constant and delightful taste experience in the real tradition of quality. Quality starts with handselecting the best ingredients and processing them in world class manufacturing units . Superior R&D experience and a strict quality approach ensures that you always get the same distinctive Paan Masala that has millions of pan masala fans across the India.
